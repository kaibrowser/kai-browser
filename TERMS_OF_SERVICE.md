2. Revenue Sharing & Monetization
2.1 Revenue Share Model

For paid extensions sold through the Kai Marketplace:

    Developer receives: 70% of the sale price (after payment processing fees)
    Kai Marketplace receives: 30% of the sale price (after payment processing fees)
    Payment processing fees are deducted before the revenue split

Example: If an extension sells for $10.00:

    Payment processor fee: ~$0.50 (varies by processor)
    Net revenue: $9.50
    Developer receives: $6.65 (70% of $9.50)
    Kai Marketplace receives: $2.85 (30% of $9.50)
